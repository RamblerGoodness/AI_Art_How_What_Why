Overview

Purpose

I have been playing around with AI art generation so i want to use this site to show off what i've made so far. I also want to use this site to educate people about how AI art works. I will also use this site to show off my art made by AI and the technics I've learned. I will also use this site to show people how they can get started.

Audience

Any one who might be interested in AI generated art. This has been a trendy topic as of late so I'm sure lots of people will be interested. My target is people who are interested as a hobby. I wont be able to show them how to make AI art in a professional setting or use. This is more to show off and get people interested in AI art.

To best cater to the intended audience I should:
Give a brief explanation to AI art and what kind of AI model I use.
Give lots of examples of art that I generated and the prompts i used.
Show people how they can get started using AI to make there own art.

Questions

I hope I can answer

What is AI art?
What is an AI art model?
How do I choose one?
How can I get started making AI art?

Why will they come

I hope to create more then just a tutorial. I want to show off my art and the art of others. I want to show people how they can get started making AI art. This page will be more then just a reddit post or a youtube video. I want to make a site that is easy to navigate and looks good. I will make a tutorial that is easy to follow and have all the information they will need to get started in on place. No more searching through reddit posts and youtube videos to find relevant information.
